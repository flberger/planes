Class p.g.FadingContainer(Container):

Part of planes.gui View In Hierarchy

Known subclasses: planes.gui.tmb.TMBFadingContainer

A Container that, once visible, will fade out and destroy itself.

Additional attributes:

FadingContainer.display_duration
    The number of calls to update() that the FadingContainer will be
    displayed. Will be decremented in FadingContainer.update().

FadingContainer.alpha_steps
    A list of decreasing alpha values to be applied to the
    Surface of the FadingContainer, computed from fade_duration
    in FadingContainer.__init__().
Method __init__ Initialise.
Method update Call Plane.update(), then decrement FadingContainer.display_duration and destroy when len(self.alpha_steps) has reached zero.
Method render Call Plane.render(), the pop the first value from self.alpha_steps and apply it as alpha.
